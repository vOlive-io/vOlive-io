Check out my games

Garden (UNDER CONSTRUCTION! PHASE 6/10)

A relaxed gardening game, with tons of achievements, 4 seasons, and 50+ plants! Look in wonder as you grow your own oacis, and maybe even build a semi-illegal bio experimental gardening lab.

Kiss, Mary, Kill (UNDER CONSTRUCTION! PHASE 4/10)

The classic game of kiss, marry, kill, where you have to pick one character to kiss, one character to marry, and one to kill. This low-stakes, silly, "wait, can I redo?" kind of game will leave you wanting to know how a simple game got so fun!

Extreme Collectionist (UNDER CONSTRUCTION! PHASE 1/10)

Have you ever been in a store and gone: "Oh my God, I want that", and: "What about that one?", and: "I want this one too, but I can't get them all, I wish they were free", right? Well, in this game, you can have it all, and for free! The goal of extreme collecting is to gather as many items as possible. You do this by clicking "The Big Bag" How much can you collect?

Loathsome Legacy (UNDER CONSTRUCTION! PHASE 3/10)

A game full of rage and loathing, as the name implies, builds your horrid reputation by worshiping the all-mighty god of hellfire and hatred: Silly Boop! Each time you click Silly Boop's face, he'll give me one Legacy point. Spend these on devices that will help you and your mission to become the most hated being of all!
